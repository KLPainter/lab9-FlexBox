Chocolate Pizza

POSTED ON 15 DEC 2013 / DESSERTS

For the fig-swirl: Melt putter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the mild is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in teh fridge and wait until the fix mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instruction, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding marcarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1 lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 heavy cream
  • 1/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey-roasted pecans, roughly chopped
